"Shapes R us”

An experience of learning patterns on to this project, the interdiction how to use the laser cutter. We place simple modular and make an individual part that can be put together with the paper examples. Brainstorming mockup design with board or paper, learning different types of fasteners. Then we create in illustration 4 side goldilocks design to connect our project. The last project is the laser cutting group design ” Shapes R us”, using Illustrator and laser cutting on thin wood to create a children’s toy of expending children’s imagery and creativity. With slot to sild to another piece pointy part of the shape on order to create a bunch of connecting shape and hang on the hangers.
